- BREAKING CHANGES:
    - The `gemseo.core.mdo_functions` package was renamed to `gemseo.core.functions`.
    - The `gemseo.core.mdo_functions.mdo_function` module was renamed to `gemseo.core.functions.array_function`.
    - The `gemseo.core.mdo_functions.mdo_linear_function` module was renamed to `gemseo.core.functions.linear_function`.
    - The `gemseo.core.mdo_functions.mdo_quadratic_function` module was renamed to `gemseo.core.functions.quadratic_function`.
    - The `MDOFunction` class was renamed to `ArrayFunction`.
    - The `MDOLinearFunction` class was renamed to `LinearFunction`.
    - The `MDOQuadraticFunction` class was renamed to `QuadraticFunction`.
    - The `mdo_function` argument of `Offset`, `ConvexLinearApprox`, `FunctionRestriction` and `SetPtFromDatabase` were renamed to `array_function`.
