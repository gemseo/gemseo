- `DistributionFactory.create_marginal_distribution` was removed;
  use `DistributionFactory().create_from_settings` instead,
  e.g. `DistributionFactory().create_from_settings(SPJointDistribution_Settings(marginal_settings=[OTNormalDistribution_Settings(), [OTUniformDistribution_Settings()]))` instead.
- `DistributionFactory.create_joint_distribution` was removed;
  use `DistributionFactory().create_from_settings` instead,
  e.g. `DistributionFactory().create_from_settings(OTNormalDistribution_Settings())` instead.
