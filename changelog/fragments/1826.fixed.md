`XLSDiscipline.__del__` no longer raises `AttributeError` when garbage collected after a failed initialization (for example, the `ImportError` path triggered by a missing `xlwings`).
An `XLSDiscipline` can now be garbage collected before interpreter exit. The previous `atexit.register` callback held a strong reference to the discipline via a bound method, keeping it (and its Excel process) alive for the lifetime of the process.
