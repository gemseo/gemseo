`Discipline.local_data` and `IO.data` are deprecated; use `Discipline.input_data` and `Discipline.output_data` (or `IO.input_data` and `IO.output_data`) instead. A new helper `IO.get(name)` reads from `IO.output_data` first, falling back to `IO.input_data`, matching the output-overrides-input semantics of `IO.get_merged_data()`. The deprecated attributes still work — they return a fresh union of the two stores — but emit a `DeprecationWarning`. Removal version: TBD.
